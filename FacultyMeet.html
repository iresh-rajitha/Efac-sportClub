Faculty Meet history

The Inter-faculty meet is usually held annually with all the faculties of University of Ruhuna competed with each other in 18 sports (Cricket, Volleyball, Carrom, Badminton, Taekwondo, Football, Table Tennis, Weightlifting, Baseball, Basketball, Road Race, Hockey, Chess, Swimming, Netball, Elle, Karate and Athletics).

A completed meet is organized in 2018 for the last time. Sports team of Faculty of Engineering was able to win 1st place in men’s category with 134 points, 3 rd place in women’s category with 68 points and overall championship in this event with 202 total points. This is the first time the 200 point mark was surpassed by a faculty in an inter-faculty meet of University of Ruhuna
